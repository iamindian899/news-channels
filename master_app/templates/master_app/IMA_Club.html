IMA CLUBS

Soldiering is the profession of the valiant and adventure is the corollary of bravery. The aim of adventure training at IMA is to develop key leadership traits such as mental and physical robustness, teamwork, decision making and problem solving abilities. It also provides the GC an opportunity to familiarize with remote areas, interact with cadets from other service training institutions, learn adventure sports and provides them a pleasant change from the rigorous schedule at the academy. Adventure training serves to bring out the hidden aspects of a man's character which may have been overlooked during routine structured training schedules.

 

Outdoor Club

GC is encouraged to participate in extra curricular activities so as to develop creativity and interest in hobbies. The following outdoor club activities are conducted:-

 

Shooting Club : A large no of GC attended this Club. The interest shown by the GCs in the Skill at Arms Club must be kept alive at all times. Improvements are an ongoing process. The GC of this club vies the adventure hike and have been able to achieve the desired enthusiasm for pursuing this sport.

D&M Club : This club imparts driving and maintenance training to GC. Novice drivers exhibit a lot of josh and many of them have already mastered the art of driving. The club does have its fair share of humorous incidents like mistaking reverse gear for fwd. The club is being conducted in a very program manner including introduction to heavy vehicle and working models. It is one of the most popular outdoor clubs with the GC.

Archery : The Archery Club is yet another popular club wherein the GC are taught the basics as well as the finer aspects of archery and provided with bow and arrows.

Golf Club : A few GC familiar with the game, join the club, however the majority are beginners with equipment available in the Golf Club. Keen enthusiasm is displayed by the GC and their efforts to learn the game are intense and at times hilarious.

Squash Club : IMA is one of the few institutions which has a large number of squash courts. While this club has offered GC the opportunity to learn and excel in a game, it has also helped in the overall development of self confidence of these GC. The deg of proficiency achieved by GC in such a short time is commendable.

Rafting Club : Rafting is a good means to inculcate the spirit of adventure, camaraderie and competition amongst the GCs. This Club is a big hit with the enthusiastic GC.

Scuba Diving Club : Scuba Diving Club has been functioning inside the premises of IMA at the Salaria Swimming Club. The training imparted to them includes:-

 (aa) Equipment handling
(ab) Basic diving technique

(ac) Cleaning air pressure
(ad) Use of compass inside water
(ae) Use of fins for traversing beneath water surface
(af) Buddy diving

Rock Climbing, Trekking & Mountaineering : This Club has been functioning on every Wed and is conducted on the artificial rock face inside IMA. Members of this Club were put through a short capsule in NIM Uttarkashi. A mountain expedition to Rudu Gaira (5819 Mtrs) in the Garhwal Himalayas was accomplished in May / Jun 2012.

Riding & Polo Club : The Riding and Polo outdoor club at the Indian Military Academy serves as the only venue for good and keen horse riders, improve their skills and enhance their riding capabilities. It has voluntary membership from all the IMA GCs and ACC Cadets. This Club has been contributing immensely to the End of Term Multi Activity Display where riders participate in the Horse show jumping and Tent pegging.

Tennis Club : The Tennis & Squash clubs are fully functional on all Club days. The GC and the Cadets are kept busy developing their skills at wielding the racquets at the nets and walls. All beginners are made to prac at the wall and subsequently with each other. Once the skill improves, they are pitted against better players. The GC display tremendous enthusiasm and interest in picking up the game. They are supplied with racquets and balls for this purpose. Flood lights and synthetic turf of international std has been installed on the tennis courts.

Aero Modeling Club : Aero modeling club was intro in IMA in Spring Term 2005. Theoretical and Practical know how is being imparted to the GC with regard to the handling of the flying machines. Minor repairs were undertaken by GC themselves. The response has been overwhelming and the club is functioning at full swing. The Aero modeling club has always put up a commendable show during the End of Term Multi activity Display. Models of helicopters and UAV fitted with cameras are being procured by the Club so that real time info in ex areas can be obtained by the GC.

Indoor Clubs

Five Indoor Clubs provide ample opportunities to the GC to explore and harness their innate potential, creativity and display of talent in respective fields. Highlights of the few are:-

Journalism Club : Bring out the monthly IMA news letter 'Tons View' and IMA Journal during each term.

Arts Club: The artistic works of GC in oil, water color, charcoal, glass paintings and pencil sketches are displayed during End of Term Exhibitions that are witnessed by the Reviewing Officers and Parents of the GC.

Music & Drama Club: An End of Term English Play and Musical Evening is organized by the GC showcasing their acting and singing talents that is witnessed by parents of the passing out GC.
