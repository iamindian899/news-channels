Rail Connections

Meter gauge railway lines to Ratlam (Delhi-Kota-Mumbai) and to Khandwa (Delhi-Bhopal-Mumbai) connect Mhow. The nearest railway station (NRS) (broad gauge station is Indore and is located 23 Kms from Mhow. Booking of vehicles/ trunks/ unaccompanied should be done up to Mhow.
